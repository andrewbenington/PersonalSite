    • ShareQuote: A Personal Project

    I've been working on a social-media-like platform for keeping track of funny quotes people say and tagging them in them. I used Flutter so the app will be available on iOS and Android, and Google Firebase for authentication and as a database. I wrote the cloud functions in JavaScript using Node.js. Working on this project has given me valuable experience in database design, caching information, and handling user-to-user interaction I hope make the app available for beta testers in the next few weeks.

    Google Doc Syncing

    Another feature of the app is that it can sync up with a Google Document of quotes in addition to ones created directly by users. To do this, I have a function that parses the raw HTML of a published google doc, and recognizes quotes and the people they are attributed to.
